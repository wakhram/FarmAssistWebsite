FarmAssist

We create an environment for successful development and cooperation in agriculture

Our services are available in FarmAssist app

FarmAssist is a universal solution that helps increase the efficiency of agricultural activities. By combining news, finance, a knowledge base, and a community, the application creates an environment for successful development and cooperation in agriculture.

Functions for Improving Agribusiness

Login/Registration

Each user will have their own personal account to manage their farm.

News Block

Current weather and the latest news about the agricultural sector from the world, Central Asia, and Kazakhstan.

Finance

All your financial operations in one place.

Knowledge Base

More than 1600 articles about agriculture and animal husbandry.

Community

Communicate, ask questions, share useful information and life hacks with other farmers.
